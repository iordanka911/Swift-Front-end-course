Planets

Solar system

The solar system comprises 8 planets, approximately 170 natural planetary satellites (moons), and countless asteroids, meteorites, and comets. There are more planets than stars in our galaxy. The current count orbiting our star: eight. The inner, rocky planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars. NASA's newest rover — Perseverance — landed on Mars on Feb. 18, 2021. The outer planets are gas giants Jupiter and Saturn and ice giants Uranus and Neptune. Beyond Neptune, a newer class of smaller world s called dwarf planets reign, including longtime favorite Pluto.
